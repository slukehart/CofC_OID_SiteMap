Student Affairs

Title: ALANA Interactive Wellness Event

Liaison: Rochelle Johnson

Date: Thursday, February 27

Description: The ALANA Interactive Wellness Event will connect the College of Charleston African American, Asian American, Latin American, and Native American students through stimulating activities surrounding health, environmental and financial wellness to support students' emotional wellbeing. The event will offer an array of activities, giveaways, and dining dollars, with a food truck providing refreshments.
