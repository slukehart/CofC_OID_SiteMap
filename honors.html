Honor’s College

Title 1: Palentine’s Art Night

Liaison: Jennifer Cavalli

Date: mid-February

Description: Bring a friend and put your artistic mark on a collaborative mural in the media space on the first floor of Berry Residence Hall. The theme of the night will be "Celebrating Community, Building Belonging." This is an opportunity to relax, celebrate, and exercise creativity to represent and acknowledge all members of our community, both inside and outside of Honors, and on and off CofC's campus.

Title 2: Affinity Group Listening Session

Date: March 18

Description: Collaborating with Honors Admissions during "The Honors Experience," an accepted student event being held on March 18, 2022, the student-led Honors Outreach Committee is working with Honors staff to form affinity breakout groups. Current students will moderate discussions with prospective students and then Honors will hold a listening luncheon with the students leading the affinity groups. Honors will use the qualitative data from the listening session to supplement the Honors D.E.I. Survey, which we administer to students bi-annually.
